About Me

"Those who God bless, man cannot curse."

I wake up every morning blessed to have another day left to live. Another day to work towards making a difference in this world. I have loved helping people ever since I was little. Whether it was by teaching my fellow classmates something they did not understand in class, or creating a planner to help someone organize their life, or simply being silly to put a smile on a stranger's face; nothing makes me happier and feeling more accomplished than positively affecting my community. I found that technology is a great way for me to extend my reach of assistance and because of this I pursued a career in Computer Science. In addition, I acquired a minor in math because numbers have always been a passion of mine. I am always looking for new opportunties to grow and develop both as a programmer and entrepreneur so if you have a software developer role to fill or an idea for a business feel free to contact me.
